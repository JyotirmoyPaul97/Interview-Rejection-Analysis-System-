**Turn every rejection into your next power-up.**  
A gamified AI platform that analyzes your interview rejections, uncovers patterns, and helps you level up your career with engaging visuals and smooth animations.

---

## ✨ Experience It Like a Game

IRAS transforms the painful process of interview rejections into an immersive, motivating experience:

- **You are the Hero** – Build your character through consistent improvement
- **Rejections = Boss Battles** – Analyze and defeat them with data
- **Progress = XP & Rewards** – Earn levels, badges, and unlock new abilities
- **Skill Tree** – Visually upgrade your professional skills

---

## 🎨 UI/UX Highlights

- **Cyberpunk-Neon + Dark Theme** with smooth gradients and glowing accents
- **Game-inspired HUD** (Dashboard with XP bar, level badge, and streak counter)
- **Cinematic Animations** throughout the app for a premium feel
- Fully responsive design with consistent game-like interactions on mobile

### Key Animated Features

- **Hero Landing Page** — Floating 3D icons, particle effects, and glowing neon title with glitch animation
- **Level Up Sequences** — Full-screen celebration with shockwave, badge spin, and XP burst
- **Rejection Analysis Report** — Cinematic reveal with flying data cards, glowing skill tree connections, and particle explosions
- **Interactive 3D Skill Tree** — Nodes light up with electric arcs and smooth camera movement
- **Progress Bars & Charts** — Fluid animations resembling health bars and combo meters
- **Micro-interactions** — Hover effects, button presses, and satisfying transitions using Framer Motion
- **Confetti & Reward Explosions** on achievements and successful analyses

All animations are buttery smooth (60 FPS), performance-optimized, and respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
